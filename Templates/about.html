About IndiaPropertyHub

IndiaPropertyHub is your one-stop portal for discovering and managing real estate listings across India. Whether you’re looking to buy, rent, or list a property, our platform makes it fast and intuitive.

Key Features

  • Powerful Search & Filters: Search by keywords, city, property type, amenities or budget.
  • Buy & Rent Modes: Switch between sale and rent to see only relevant listings.
  • High-Quality Listings: View multiple images, property details including area, RERA registration, and price history.
  • User Accounts: Secure login for agents/admins to create, update, or delete listings.
  • Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on mobile, tablet, and desktop.
  • Future-Ready: Easily extendable for adding workflows like bookings, virtual tours, and more.

How It Works

  1. Use the Home page to browse the latest listings.
  2. Apply filters to narrow down by location, type or budget.
  3. Click on any listing to view full details and high-resolution images.
  4. Agents/Admins: Login to access the Manage panel and maintain your portfolio.
